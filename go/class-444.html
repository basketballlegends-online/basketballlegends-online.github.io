How to Play Basket Bros

Whether you're a seasoned gamer or just looking for quick fun, Basket Bros is incredibly easy to pick up:

Single Player Mode

Practice your skills against the computer using just your keyboard arrow keys to move and the space bar to shoot.

2-Player Mode

Challenge a friend on the same keyboard! Player 1 uses WASD to move and F to shoot, while Player 2 uses the arrow keys and M.

Want to become a Basket Bros champion? Try these pro tips:
